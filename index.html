Our Toddler Lessons (for ages 24 to 36 months)

At Little Splashes, our toddler swimming lessons focus on developing essential swimming skills in a fun, engaging environment. Using sensory toys, nursery rhymes, and songs, we create a stimulating experience that promotes physical, emotional, and cognitive growth.

Our classes include playful activities to improve coordination, balance, and water confidence, with a focus on independent movement and basic stroke fundamentals. Through circuit-based lessons, toddlers rotate between activities to keep learning fresh and exciting. Songs and activities change every few months to maintain interest and continuous development. With experienced instructors, your child will build water safety skills while growing emotionally and socially.

Key benefits for your child

Development of Swimming Fundamentals

Introduction to basic stroke techniques, such as reach-and-pull movements, long-leg flutter kicks, and single-arm streamlining.

Brain and Motor Skills Development

Water activities can stimulate brain growth and improve fine motor skills and gross motor coordination.

Emotional Growth

Learning in the water builds emotional resilience. Our program emphasizes communication, patience, teamwork, and problem-solving.

Class Programme Overview

  1. Independent Water Movement: We begin by teaching toddlers to move confidently in the water. We emphasize balance, safety, and coordination through guided movements and engaging activities.
  2. Stroke Fundamentals: We introduce the basics of swim strokes, such as reach & pull, long legs flutter kick, and one-arm streamlining. This helps toddlers to form proper swimming habits. 
  3. Emotional and Character Building: We help to develop self-esteem, communication, and patience with a circuit-based and safety swim programmes.
  4. Interactive Learning: To keep toddlers engaged, our lessons incorporate engaging songs, toys, and activities that are refreshed every 4 months to keep toddlers excited about learning.

Our Instructors

At Little Splashes Aquatics, our instructors are carefully selected based on their qualifications, experience, and passion for teaching toddlers. Each instructor undergoes extensive training, with international certifications like AUSTSWIM, SSI, STA and intensive 500+ hours of in-house training. Our low student-to-instructor ratio ensures personalized attention, allowing us to adapt lessons to each child’s progress and needs.
